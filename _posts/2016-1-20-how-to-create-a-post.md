Creating posts on the blog is easy.
1. Click `_posts`,you'll see posts set up by date and title.
2. Click `new file`. 
3. Style the title like `YYYY-MM-DD-name-of-post.ext`. 
4. Include this at the top of the page: 
5. `---
  layout: post
  title: "example"
  date:0000-0-00
  categories: staq
  ---`
And you're all set!
